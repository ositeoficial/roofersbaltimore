Diverse Roofing Materials​

Our contractors specialize in working with a wide range of roofing materials, carefully selecting those best suited for Baltimore’s environmental factors to ensure long-lasting durability and performance.
Metal Roofing

Metal roofing options include shingles, steel panels, and standing seam panels. Valued for its durability, fire resistance, lightweight design, and low maintenance, metal roofing typically lasts around 40 years.

Slate Roofing

Slate is a premium roofing material known for its remarkable longevity, lasting up to 100 years. Composed of natural stone, it endures extreme temperatures and fire while enhancing the visual appeal of your home.

Asphalt Shingles

Asphalt roofing offers a balance of affordability and visual appeal, available in strip, dimensional, and luxury styles. It delivers durability while providing a variety of design options to suit different preferences.

Wood Roofing

Wood roofing, crafted from cedar, pine, and redwood, provides excellent insulation and a timeless rustic look. While it typically lasts around 30 years, it requires regular maintenance and is not ideal for regions prone to fires.

Rubber Roofing

Known as EPDM, rubber roofing comes in both shingles and rolls. This cost-effective and eco-friendly choice is ideal for flat roofs, providing a lifespan of up to 50 years with minimal maintenance.
